# Play Store Assets Guide

## Graphic Assets Checklist

### App Icon
- **Format**: 32-bit PNG (with alpha)
- **Dimensions**: 512px by 512px
- **Max file size**: 1024KB
- **Requirement**: High-resolution version of the app launcher icon.

### Feature Graphic
- **Format**: JPEG or 24-bit PNG (no alpha)
- **Dimensions**: 1024px by 500px
- **Requirement**: Used in the Play Store app "Featured" section. Avoid placing text near the edges or cut-off points. 
- **Theme**: Use the app's primary purple color (#6650A5) and dark/light variants.

### Phone Screenshots
- **Format**: JPEG or 24-bit PNG (no alpha)
- **Dimensions**: Min 320px, Max 3840px (2:1 aspect ratio max)
- **Quantity**: 2 to 8 screenshots
- **Recommended Screens**:
  1.  **Note List (Home)**: Showing organized notes.
  2.  **Chat Interface**: Showing "Chat with your notes" and AI response.
  3.  **Note Detail**: Showing markdown editing.
  4.  **Files/Settings**: Showing model management or settings.
  5.  **Dark Mode**: Showing at least one screen in dark theme.

### 7-inch & 10-inch Tablet Screenshots
- **Format**: JPEG or 24-bit PNG (no alpha)
- **Requirement**: Required to make the app eligible for the "Designed for tablets" list. Upload screenshots taken on tablet emulators.

## Video (Optional)
- YouTube video URL showing gameplay/usage (landscape).

## Tips
- Use the "Demo Mode" on Android devices to clean up the status bar (full battery, 12:00 time, full signal) before taking screenshots.
- Ensure text in screenshots is readable.
